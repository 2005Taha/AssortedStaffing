FAQ

Common questions

How fast can we hire?

Most teams are recruited, vetted, and onboarded within a few weeks of the discovery call, depending on role complexity.

How is quality ensured?

Every hire goes through structured vetting, and ongoing performance monitoring is built into how we manage your team — not left to chance.

What industries do you serve?

We work with startups, SaaS companies, agencies, construction firms, accounting practices, healthcare businesses, and e-commerce brands, among others.

Can teams scale later?

Yes. Teams are built to grow — adding roles or headcount is a matter of extending your existing managed team, not starting over.

How does pricing work?

Pricing is based on the roles and team size you need. You get one predictable monthly cost, discussed in detail on your discovery call.

What happens if someone leaves?

We manage the transition and recruit a replacement, so your operations continue with minimal disruption.
